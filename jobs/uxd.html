The Springer Development Team

User Experience Designer

Full time, Springer-Verlag London Ltd

Introducing ourselves

Springer is a world-class international publisher. We understand the written word, and our design reflects this in every detail: our aesthetic is modern, clean and well-structured, our typography beautiful but readable first and foremost.

We are a multi-disciplinary team working in a fast-paced and collaborative environment, responsible for designing, building and testing Springer’s new digital platform. We’re building an enterprise-class web application, and delivering a first-rate user experience is essential. We’re seeking a senior user experience practitioner to join our design team: a small, collaborative discipline where we like to think aloud, experiment and fail fast.

We build our software iteratively, so you’ll be able to see new work go live to over four million visitors around the globe every week. Our teams are cross-discipline: you’ll work with business analysts and stakeholders to understand requirements, pair with user experience designers and front-end developers to continuously evolve proposed solutions, and you’ll be able to justify your rationale to development teams when necessary. Our core values are simplicity, openness, enthusiasm and excellence.

Job duties

You’ll be expert in information architecture and interaction design for complex online systems, and experienced in planning, conducting, and interpreting usability studies. Our ideal candidate would be a confident generalist with knowledge across the whole ux spectrum: we’d love someone who can critique design, offer different insights on front-end principles, and advise on content strategy. This team is tasked with integrating Springer’s key online offerings into our new platform. We want someone with the enthusiasm to advocate the user experience across the organisation: this role is yours to shape.

Job requirements

Essential

  • An advocate empathy for real users and evidence-driven, iterative design
  • Proven understanding of the practical application of user-centred design and building engaging experiences
  • Understands designing interfaces across multiple platforms and devices
  • Champions user experience within an agile product development team
  • Enjoys collaborating across disciplines and departments
  • Proven experience of sketching and visualising in order to communicate your ideas, the quicker the better
  • Enjoys leading collaborative design sessions and coaching non-designers to think creatively
  • Highly detail-orientated, self-sufficient and delivery focused
  • Friendly and adaptable, an excellent communicator, and believe in working transparently

Desirable

  • Excited by our business domain: science, technology and business publishing
  • Any experience of agile ux would certainly help you hit the ground running
