The Springer Development Team

Business Analyst

Full time, Springer-Verlag London Ltd

We’re looking for a great Business Analyst to join our platform development team in London. We need someone with a passion for building great online products, an eye for detail and experience with working in an agile development environment.

Job duties

  • Work with internal (and potentially external) stakeholders to understand goals & vision, and identify business needs
  • Interview stakeholders to generate functional requirements for development teams
  • Writing story cards and narratives that are clear, concise, comprehensible, and that can be turned in to code and tests by the project Team
  • Ensure that business needs, requirements and their alignment with “big picture” goals and vision are communicated to development teams through effective story writing
  • Provide business analysis support to Developers and QA team members during card development and change/implementation teams following sign off to ensure maximum success upon launch of the new system
  • On-going user acceptance testing to support our short, iterative release cycles
  • Identifying and resolving issues with missing stories and conflicting requirements
  • Unearth the root cause(s) of pain points, validating the business opportunity or problem as an opportunity for a software solution
  • Facilitate workshops and gain buy in for solution
  • Collaborate with Product Owner, Business Sponsors, SMEs, Testers and Development groups
  • Proactively identify and resolve analysis, scope, requirements and process issues
  • Demo system development to key stakeholders

Job requirements

Essential:

  • A broad understanding of business processes and drivers
  • Demonstrated experience working successfully with teams to deliver software
  • Experience prioritizing competing demands and obtaining buy-in from stakeholders
  • Demonstrated ability to manage and resolve conflict with stakeholders
  • Experience modelling business processes using a variety of tools and techniques
  • Strong communication skills and facilitation experience
  • Demonstrated ability to understand and explain issues from both a technical and a business functional point of view
  • An understanding of Agile delivery methodologies (e.g. Scrum and XP)

Desirable:

  • Experience in the publishing industry
  • Familiarity with wireframe and mockup tools (Balsamiq, Axure, Photoshop).
