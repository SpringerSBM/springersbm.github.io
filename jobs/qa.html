The Springer Development Team

QA Engineers

Full time, Springer-Verlag London Ltd

We’re looking for a QA engineers who not only have the technical ability, passion for quality and very good attention to details but also the necessary people skills to be effective in an agile team. We are driven by innovation, technology, passion for quality & delivery and team spirit. Using lots of test automation, we reduced manual regression testing to just one hour per week; meaning you will never be bored doing the same things.

Job duties

  • Review user stories to ensure they are complete and identify the requirements for test data
  • Participate in story kick-offs, joining developers, BAs, UX designers and architects to ensure the business requirement is fully understood
  • Participate in story demos to ensure that all acceptance criteria have been met, automated tests implemented and any test data required has been delivered
  • Test completed stories and obtain business sign-off so the artefact can be deployed to production
  • Take an active part in system test automation and manual regression testing
  • Take part in project inceptions to understand upcoming projects and provide QA insight
  • Report and follow up on issues

Job requirements

Essential:

  • Proven experience in Agile Development; desirably Test Driven Development
  • Extensive test experience
  • Proven experience of scripted/coded test automation (not record & play)
  • Solid knowledge of Selenium RC/Webdriver
  • Test automation at unit, functional, integration and system level
  • Good SQL knowledge
  • Proven knowledge of Java, Scala, C# or comparable language as well as Unix
  • Attention to detail
  • Passion for quality and ability to speak to all members of the business
  • Proactive in identifying and bringing issues to attention
  • Excellent communication skills

Desirable:

  • Continous Integration
  • Agile certified testing experience
  • Cloud test automation
  • Media/publishing experience

Working here

We build our software iteratively, so you’ll be able to see new work go live to over four million visitors around the globe every week. Our teams are cross-discipline and you’ll work with business analysts and stakeholders to understand requirements, pair with developers to review automated unit & functional tests, and collaborate with devops to understand technical implications of configuration changes and test if necessary. Our core values are simplicity, openness, enthusiasm and excellence.

This position is based in our London office, and reports to the QA Manager.

Please send your CV and covering letter, stating salary expectations and quoting the job reference (SPR/QA/C)to: HR, Floor 6, 236 Gray’s Inn Road, London, WC1X 8HB, or by email to: ukjobs@springer.com.

Springer offers a competitive salary and benefits package including 25 days holiday plus bank holidays, contributory pension scheme, life assurance, discounted gym membership, cycle to work scheme, childcare vouchers and an employee support programme.
