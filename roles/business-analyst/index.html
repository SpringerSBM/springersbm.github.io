{{page.lead-in-what}}…

  • work with stakeholders to understand our goals & vision, and identify business needs.
  • pair with quality analysts and user experience designers to write stories that are clear, concise and comprehensible.
  • communicate to the development team how the requirements are aligned with the business needs and “big picture” goals and vision.
  • collaborate and provide business analysis support to the product owner, business sponsors, SMEs and testers during development.
  • identify and resolve issues with missing stories and conflicting requirements.
  • unearth the root cause(s) of pain points, and validate business opportunities and problems.
  • facilitate workshops with a range of stakeholders in the business.
